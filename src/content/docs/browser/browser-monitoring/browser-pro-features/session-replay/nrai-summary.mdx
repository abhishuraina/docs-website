The New Relic AI helps you to summarise Session replay captures and interactions on your web app and map other telemetry data to help you understand your user's journeys.

<Steps>

      <Step>
        Go to <DNT>**[one.newrelic.com > All Capabilities](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Browser**</DNT>.
      </Step>

      <Step>
        Select your browser app.
      </Step>

      <Step>
        In the left-hand menu, scroll down and click <DNT>**Session replay**</DNT>.
      </Step>

      <Step>
        Click into each replay to view session details.

         <img
           title="Session replay"
           alt="screenshot of the session replay page in browser"
           src="/images/browser_screenshot-full_Session-replay-page.webp"
         />
      </Step>

      <Step>
        Click the ellipsis icon for the <DNT>**Event trail**</DNT>.
      </Step>

      <Step>
        Click <DNT>**Summarize (Ask AI)**</DNT>. A summary of the session is generated on the focus panel.

        <img
           title="Session replay"
           alt="screenshot of the session replay page in browser"
           src="/images/session-replay-summary.webp"
         />
      </Step>
    
</Steps>
