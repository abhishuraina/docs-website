    With Streaming Video & Ads intelligence, monitor and optimize video and ad streaming performance with intelligent insights into quality of experience metrics. In today's competitive media landscape, delivering an exceptional viewing experience is crucial for achieving success. Our platform provides media and entertainment companies with full stack visibility needed to improve playback experience and viewer engagement. Seamlessly integrated into the New Relic platform, Streaming Video & Ads intelligence enables you to:

    * Monitor video and ad QoE metrics and set up alerts.
    * Correlate data across your entire stack to pinpoint the root cause of video and ad playback issues.
    * Track user engagement and video/ad playback completion rates.
    * Gain visibility into user sessions to troubleshoot playback issues.

    By leveraging these features, you can maximize viewer engagement, boost ad revenues and minimize churn through continuous optimization of playback experience.
